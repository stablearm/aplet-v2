# Aplet Product Blueprint

Version: 1.0

---

# Product

Name: Aplet

Category: Telegram Business Operating System

---

# Mission

Build the most complete platform for Telegram growth, sponsorship, monetization and publisher management.

---

# Target Users

- Publisher
- Advertiser
- Admin

---

# Core Products

The product consists of the following modules.

These modules are the source of truth.

- Dashboard
- Campaigns
- Publishers
- Content Bot
- Marketplace
- Wallet
- Revenue
- Analytics
- Subscription
- Settings

No additional modules may be introduced without explicit approval.

---

# Business Goals

Allow publishers to:

- Connect Telegram channels
- Connect Telegram bots
- Lock premium content
- Join sponsorship campaigns
- Track earnings
- Withdraw revenue

Allow advertisers to:

- Create campaigns
- Manage budget
- Track campaign performance
- Purchase real Telegram members

Allow administrators to:

- Manage the ecosystem
- Moderate publishers
- Review campaigns
- Monitor payments

---

# Product Principles

The product is a business operating system.

Not a marketing website.

Not a social network.

Not an analytics dashboard.

Every screen must help users complete business tasks.

---

# Design Principles

Clarity before beauty.

Product before marketing.

Show instead of explain.

Numbers before decoration.

Whitespace is a component.

Minimal but powerful.

---

# Constraints

Mobile First.

RTL Native.

Persian Native.

API First.

Performance First.

Accessibility AA.

---

# Source of Truth

All future architecture, pages, navigation, components and API integration must follow this document.

Do not invent functionality.

Do not remove modules.

Do not rename modules.

Do not redesign the business model.
